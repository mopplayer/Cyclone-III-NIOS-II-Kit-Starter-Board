VGA Picture Viewer Application
==============================
This is a VGA mirrored version of the original Picture Viewer Application

Picture Viewer Application 
==========================
Takes JPEG or BMP images stored on SD Card and displays them on LCD Panel

How to use the demo
===================
1) Touch 'Forward' for next image, 'Reverse' for previous
2) Touch Play/Stop to play or stop slide
3) Touch + or - to adjust delay
4) Touch Hide to hide, touch anywhere to unhide

ADD YOUR PICS TO THE PICTURE VIEWER
--------------------------------
- Connect SD Card to your PC using the reader
- Copy your JPEGS to 'Image' directory on SD Card
- This example design comes with full source code
